Big's Backyard Ultra 2025, held October 18 at Lazarus Lake's farm in Bell Buckle, Tennessee, is one of the most prestigious backyard ultras in the world. Runners complete a 4.167-mile loop every hour (trail during daylight, road at night) until only one remains. Phil Gore claimed victory with 114 loops, covering over 475 miles across nearly five days.
