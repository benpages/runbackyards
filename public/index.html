What is this

Built for
the obsessed.

Backyard ultra is unlike anything else in endurance sport. One loop, 4.167 miles, every hour on the hour, until only one runner crosses the start line and nobody else does. The format creates a specific kind of athlete: patient, stubborn, calculating. A race experience that standard results pages don't capture.

BACKYARDS pulls live data from raceresult.com and turns it into something you can actually read. Lap pacing trends, DNF distribution, multi-race comparisons. For spectators tracking a friend across 72 hours. For athletes studying the field. For anyone who's looked at a results page and wanted more.
