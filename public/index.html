What is this?

Backyard ultras are a last-one-standing format: runners complete a 4.167-mile loop every hour, around the clock, until only one remains. The winner is whoever finishes a loop that no one else completes.

backyards.run pulls live results from raceresult.com and turns them into interactive charts — lap-by-lap pace lines, DNF distribution bars, and multi-race comparisons. No spreadsheets, no PDFs.
